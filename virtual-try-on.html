AI Virtual Try-On

AI-Powered Virtual Try-On Experience by MARQ AI

Product Description

Our AI Virtual Try-On platform revolutionizes the online shopping experience by enabling customers to visually try on products — from clothing and accessories to eyewear and footwear — in real time using advanced Artificial Intelligence, computer vision, and augmented reality. Powered by MARQ AI, the solution delivers photorealistic try-on experiences that boost customer confidence, reduce return rates, and drive higher conversions for fashion, beauty, and retail brands.

    Why choose our AI Virtual Try-On Platform?

  • Photorealistic Visualization: Deliver lifelike virtual try-on experiences with AI-generated imagery that accurately represents how products look on each individual customer.
  • Real-Time Body Mapping: Use advanced computer vision to map body shape, size, and posture for precise garment fitting and accurate product overlay in real time.
  • Multi-Category Support: Support virtual try-on for clothing, eyewear, footwear, jewelry, makeup, and accessories from a single unified platform.
  • Seamless Integration: Easily embed virtual try-on capabilities into your existing eCommerce website, mobile app, or in-store kiosk with minimal development effort.
  • Reduced Return Rates: Empower customers to make confident purchase decisions with accurate visual previews, significantly lowering product returns.
  • Personalized Recommendations: Combine try-on data with AI-driven style recommendations to suggest complementary products and increase average order value.

Core Virtual Try-On Modules

  • Real-Time Camera Try-On

    Enable customers to try on products live using their device camera with instant AI-powered overlay rendering.

  • 3D Body Scanning & Sizing

    Capture accurate body measurements using AI-driven 3D scanning for precise fit and size recommendations.

  • Apparel Try-On

    Allow customers to virtually try on tops, bottoms, dresses, and outerwear with realistic fabric drape and fit simulation.

  • Eyewear & Accessories Try-On

    Provide accurate virtual try-on for sunglasses, prescription glasses, watches, hats, and jewelry with face and wrist mapping.

  • Beauty & Makeup Try-On

    Let customers experiment with lipstick shades, foundation tones, eye makeup, and skincare products using AI-powered face mapping.

  • Footwear Try-On

    Enable virtual shoe try-on with accurate foot mapping, size prediction, and style visualization from multiple angles.

  • Cross-Platform Deployment

    Deploy virtual try-on on web browsers, mobile apps, AR mirrors, and in-store kiosks with a single integration.

  • Analytics & Insights Dashboard

    Track try-on engagement, conversion rates, popular products, and customer preferences with real-time analytics.

By combining cutting-edge AI, computer vision, and augmented reality, MARQ AI's Virtual Try-On platform transforms online shopping into an immersive, confidence-building experience. Customers see it, try it, and buy it — reducing returns, increasing satisfaction, and driving revenue growth for forward-thinking retail brands.

  • Up to 40% Reduction in Return Rates
