04 Hiring Process

Your Journey to MARQ AI

Our streamlined hiring process is designed to be transparent, respectful of your time, and focused on discovering the best mutual fit.

1

Apply Online

Submit your application with your resume and a brief note about why MARQ AI excites you. We review every application carefully.

2

Initial Screening

A quick 30-minute call with our talent team to understand your background, aspirations, and alignment with the role.

3

Technical Assessment

Role-specific technical rounds including live coding, system design, or case studies relevant to the position you are applying for.

4

Welcome Aboard

Meet the leadership team, receive your offer, and join our onboarding program designed to set you up for success from day one.
